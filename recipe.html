Breakfast Meals Ideas

Banana Pancakes

To make banana pancakes you'll need:

  • 2 Ripe Bananas
  • 2/3 cup Rolled Oats
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
Method:
  1. Squish the two bananas in a bowl
  2. Add two eggs to the squished bananas
  3. Add 2/3 blended oats (oats flour)
  4. Add 1/2tsp of vanilla extract to the mixture

Oats

To make the best Oats you'll need:

  • Oats
  • Vanilla Milk/Oat Milk
  • Greek Youghurt
  • Strawberries
  • Blueberries
Method:
  1. Pour ratio 1:3 (Oats:Milk) in the pan
  2. Wait till you see the milk boil
  3. Pour oats on your boal
  4. Add desired amount of Greek Youghurt
  5. Add desired fruits

Crepes with fruit

To make Crepes with Fruit you'll need:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup Oat milk
  • 1/2cup water
  • 2tbsp Vegetable oil
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 cup whole-wheat flour
  • Greek Youghurt
  • Strawberries and Berries
Methods:
  1. Pour two eggs to the bowl
  2. Add 1/2cu of Oat milk or any low fat milk
  3. Pour 1/2cup water to the bowl
  4. Pour 2tbsp of vegetable oil
  5. Pour a pinch of salt
  6. After mixing throughout each step add a cup of wheat flour to the mixture
  7. Pour the final mixture to the pan i circular shape
  8. When ready spread greek youghurt to your crepes
  9. Add your chosen fruits

Lunch Meal Ideas

Stuffed Bell Pepper Rings

To make stuffed bell pepper rings you'll need:

  • 2 Bell Peppers
  • 1 Clove garlic
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1/2 Onion
  • 1 Mashroom
  • Salt and Black Pepper
  • 1/2 cup Ground Turkey
  • 1.5 tbsp Parsley
  • 1 Egg white
  • Italian seasoning
  • 4 tbsp Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzarella (optional)
Method:
  1. Pre-heat the oven to 180° C.
  2. Slice bell peppers into rings.
    Finely chop up bottomsto be added into the turkey mixture.
  3. Heat oil in a medium skillet and saute garlic onion,
    mushroom, chopped bell pepper, for about 5 minutes
    or until the onion becomes translucent.
  4. Stir in the salt, pepper.
  5. Transfer mixture to a large bowl and add in turkey, egg, parsley, italian seasoning.
  6. Mix thoroughly.
  7. Divide the mixture evenly among the 4 bell pepper rings and place on a baking sheet.
  8. Spread tomato sauce over the mini meatloafs.
  9. Bake in the oven for 30-35 minutes.
  10. Top with extra parsley and Mozzarella if you want and ENJOY!!

Veggie Spinach Roll

To make a veggie spinach roll you'll need:

  • 1 cup frozen spinach leaves
  • 3 egg whites
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 low fat mozzarella cheese
  • salt and black pepper
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 1/2 onion
  • 1/2 red bell pepper
  • 1 medium carrot shredded
  • 1.5 tbsp parsley
Method:
  1. Preheat oven to 200° C
  2. Thaw the spinach and squeeze out the water.
  3. Mix spinach, 3 egg whites, mozzarella, half the salt,
    and pepper in a mixing bowl.
  4. Place parchment paper on a baking sheet and spray with cooking spray.
  5. Move the spinach mixture to the sheet and press it flat, about 8x12 inches
    in size and roughly ½ an inch thick.
  6. Bake for 15 minutes. When done, set aside to cool on a rack.
  7. Finely chop onion and parsley. Grate the carrots.
  8. Fry the onions and garlic in a skillet with oil.
    Add carrots and parsley and let it simmer for about
    2-3 min. add the other half of the salt, and pepper and mix briefly.
  9. Flip the spinach mat and take the skillet off the heat and add an egg
    Mix it all together and spread the filling over the now cool spinach mat.
  10. Roll up the spinach mat and filling. Bake for 25 minutes.
  11. Cut it into 5 slices

Dinner Meal Ideas

Chicken Curry Rice

To make chicken curry rice you'll need:

  • 1/4 Medium onion
  • A Chicken breast
  • 1 Garlic
  • Seasonings: 1/4 tsp Ground Cumin
    Ground coriander
    Paprika
    Ground turmeric, Salt and Black Pepper
  • 1/2 cup Low sodium Chicken broth
  • 1/4 Tomato sauce
  • 1 tbsp Heavy cream
  • 1 cup brown rice
  • 1.5 tbsp Parsley
  • Coriander
Method:
  1. Chop 1/4 medium Onion
  2. Fry onion in the pan
  3. Put chopped chicken breast in the pan
  4. Chop Garlic and add it to the pan
  5. Add 1/2tsp of each seasonings listed to the pan
  6. Mix thoroughly until cooked.
  7. Pour 1/2 cup chicken broth
  8. Pour 1/4 Tomato sauce
  9. Stir until boiling
  10. Add one tbsp of heavy cream
  11. Stir until cooked
  12. Add final chicken curry to brown rice
  13. Top with extra parsley and Mozzarella if you want and ENJOY!!

Quinoa

To fry cheakpeas you'll need:

  • 1 Can of cheakpeas
  • 1 to 2 tbsp of Olive oil
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper and salt

To make quinoa you'll need:

  • 1 cup Quinoa
  • 2-3tbsp Olive oil
  • 1 cup Onion-chopped
  • 2tsp garlic paste
  • 2tsp ginger paste
  • 3/4 cup Tomato passata
  • 1/2tsp ground cumin
  • 1/4tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1+1/2 cup water
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2cup coriander/parsley
Method:
  1. To a heated pan add the cooking oil and chickpeas.
  2. Cook on medium high heat until chickpea starts to
    crisp up (it take about 2 to 3 minutes).
  3. Then add paprika, ground black pepper and salt.
  4. Fry for another 1 to 2 minutes or until the chickpeas are nicely coated with the spices.
  5. Transfer to a plate and set it aside for later.
  6. Now to the same pan add some cooking oil, onion and salt.
  7. Adding salt will release moisture from the onion and will help it cook faster so please don’t skip it.
  8. Fry on medium-high to high heat
  9. As soon as the onion starts to caramelize, add the ginger and garlic. Reduce the heat to medium low
    and fry for about 1 to 2 minutes or until fragrant.
  10. Next add the strained tomatoes/passata and let it cook for about 2 minutes, in the mean time wash
    the quinoa thoroughly with water and transfer to the pan along with ground cumin and cayenne pepper.
  11. Increase the heat to medium-high, add water and bring to a boil.
  12. Reduce the heat to low.
  13. Cover and cook on low heat for about 20 minutes or until quinoa is fully cooked.
  14. Add coriander(or presley) and the cheakpeas made before and ENJOY!

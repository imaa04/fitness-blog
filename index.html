Confidence is important

Engaging in healthy lifestyle behaviours, such as physical activity
or following a healthy diet, is associated with positive self-esteem
and mental health. It is proven that Endorphines (hormones that boost your mood)
are released when you eat healthy and exercise regurarly.
You propably thinkng how are you supposed to be happy when you are not allowed to have
your favourite food or sweating in pain during your workouts.
I had such thoughts at my starting point but getting the thought of the confidence you will
achieve after reaching your goal is what can help you throughout the process.
Thinking of why you have set such goals, the outcome and what you're going to do
with the outcome.
